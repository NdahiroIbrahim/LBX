INTRODUCTION TO LBEx

With local businesses lacking access to non-traditional funding models, LBEx aims to provide an alternative funding model that doesn't involve dilution of ownership or Equity "non-dilutive alternative"

LBEx tokenization concept

Welcome to LBX , an innovative platform designed to revolutionize how local businesses raise capital and engage investors. At LBEx, we aim to empower Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) by introducing a modern financial mechanism: the tokenization of business performance data. This groundbreaking approach enables businesses to trade their tokens like ETFs while providing investors with a transparent and accessible way to support and benefit from the growth of local enterprises. See more about ETFs here.

Features :

Tokenization of Business Performance Data

SMEs can convert their performance metrics into tradable digital tokens.

Secure Transactions

End-to-end encrypted transactions, ensuring data and financial security.

Investor Dashboards

Real-time insights and analytics for investors to track their token portfolios.

Accessible Funding

SMEs gain access to new capital sources without traditional banking restrictions.

Transparency and Accountability

Leveraging blockchain to provide transparent and immutable records of business performance.

Diverse Investment Opportunities

Investors can diversify portfolios by trading SME tokens like commodities or shares.

Regulatory Compliance

Designed to meet local and international compliance standards, ensuring trust and credibility.

Local Economic Empowerment

Strengthening the Kenyan SME ecosystem by creating new funding and investment avenues.
